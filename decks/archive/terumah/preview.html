Emotional Hook

This week is about GIVING! When you give something special to help make something beautiful, it feels SO good inside!

Teacher Script

This is Parashat Terumah! Can you say "Terumah"? Terumah! It means a special gift that you give from your HEART. This week, the Jewish people are going to build something SO special - and everyone gets to help! When we give something to help others, it makes us feel really good inside. Put your hand on your heart - that's where the good feeling is!

Visual Description

Hands lifting up colorful gifts toward golden light. Treasures include: shiny gold, purple and blue fabric, red yarn. A heart shape formed by the arrangement. Warm, glowing feeling. Community silhouettes in the background, all contributing.

Hebrew Word

转职旨专旨指
te-ru-MAH - Gift / Contribution

Torah Source (on card)

Exodus 25:2: "Tell the people to bring Me gifts - from everyone whose heart moves them."

Example for Kids (on card)

"When you share your toys, that's a terumah - a gift from your heart!"

Teacher Script

Our special Hebrew word today is TERUMAH! Can you say it? Te-ru-MAH! It means a gift - but not just any gift. A terumah is a gift you give because you WANT to, from your heart. The Torah says the people brought gifts "from everyone whose heart moved them." That means they felt the giving feeling inside! When you share something because you want to help, that's a terumah. Can you think of a terumah you gave? Let's say it again: Terumah!

When to Use

For Hebrew vocabulary focus. Can also integrate into Anchor card introduction.
